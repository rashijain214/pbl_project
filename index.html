Early Disaster Warning System using ML

"To build a predictive machine learning system capable of identifying early warning signs of natural disasters using historical environmental datasets to minimize damage and loss of life."

Problem Statement

Natural disasters such as floods, earthquakes, and cyclones cause massive damage due to delayed detection and response. Current systems are reactive rather than predictive. This project builds an ML-based early warning system to detect disaster signals in advance.

Literature Review

Existing systems rely on threshold-based detection and satellite monitoring, lacking real-time ML prediction capabilities.

Innovation

Our system integrates multiple ML models and dynamically adjusts thresholds to improve prediction accuracy and recall.

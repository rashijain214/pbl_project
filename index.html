Early Disaster Warning System using ML

To build a predictive ML system that detects early warning signals of floods, earthquakes, storms, and other disasters using historical environmental data.

Problem Statement

Current disaster systems are reactive and respond after damage occurs. There is a need for predictive ML-based systems to provide early warnings and reduce loss of life and infrastructure damage.

Literature Review

Existing systems use satellite and threshold-based monitoring with limited ML prediction capability.

Innovation

Our system integrates multiple ML models with dynamic threshold tuning for higher recall and early alerts.

System Methodology

Environmental, weather & geological dataset with preprocessing

LightGBM, RF, XGBoost, SVM, Logistic Regression, KNN
